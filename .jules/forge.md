## 2024-05-18 - Terminological inaccuracies
**Learning:** Found some amusing terminological inaccuracies like using 'stolička' (little chair/molar tooth) instead of 'stolice' (feces), and 'solárně' (solar) instead of 'solitárně' (solitary) in a medical context within `data_onco_2.js`.
**Action:** Be mindful of Czech medical terminology and homonyms when auditing content.
## 2024-05-20 - Terminological inaccuracies in Genetics
**Learning:** Found an inaccuracy where 'třetím chromozomu' (the third chromosome) was incorrectly used instead of 'nadpočetném chromozomu' (supernumerary/extra chromosome) when describing trisomies like Down syndrome (Trisomy 21). This incorrectly implied that the pathology of trisomies 21, 18, and 13 are related to chromosome 3.
**Action:** Always be mindful of numerical terms and medical terminology translation when auditing genetic contents.
