
## 2024-06-13 - Corrected terminology "konkrét" vs "konkrement" and declension of "kalich"
**Learning:** Medical terminology in Czech requires precision (e.g., "konkrement" instead of the incorrect "konkrét") and correct declension (e.g., locative plural of "kalich" is "kališích", not "kalichách").
**Action:** When updating content, verify not only the spelling but also the precise medical term and grammatical forms, especially in specialized medical data files.
