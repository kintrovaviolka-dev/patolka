## 2024-05-24 - Medical Terminology Autocorrect Risk
**Learning:** Found an embarrassing typo where "resekátu" (resection specimen) was likely autocorrected to "rektátu" (rectum/rectate) in the general pathology biopsy description. This completely changes the medical meaning from a general surgical specimen to a specific anatomical region.
**Action:** Be extra vigilant about spellcheck/autocorrect replacing specialized Czech medical terms with similar-sounding common or anatomically incorrect words when updating content.
